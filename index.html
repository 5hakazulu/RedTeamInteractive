About Us

VR Game Studio

Red Team Interactive, has established a formidable presence in the rapidly evolving landscape of virtual reality gaming. With our flagship game "Sail" already marking a significant impact in VR —taking a top 10 spot on Meta's App Lab with a 4.6 / 5 rating and surpassing 11,000 paid downloads—we're poised for exponential growth.

Our online community metrics highlight our unique brand equity with a significant presence across TikTok (67k followers), YouTube (14k subscribers), and Discord ( 5.82k members).

Backed by industry-leading firms such as Boost VC, Founders Inc., and Earthling VC, we are currently targeting a capital infusion of $700,000 through a $6 million post-money SAFE to accelerate our development. Red Team Interactive presents a compelling opportunity for investors looking to stake a claim in the promising realm of VR gaming.
